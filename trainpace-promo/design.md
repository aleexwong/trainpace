## Typography

- **Display:** Space Grotesk — headlines, stats, numbers. Weights 500/700.
- **Body:** DM Sans — paragraphs, labels, UI copy. Weights 400/500/700.
- These are the shipped brand fonts; do not substitute.

## Voice & copy

- Tagline: **"Train Smarter. Race Faster."** ("Race Faster." in emerald)
- Supporting: "Science-backed pace zones, real-world course analysis, and personalized race fueling strategies."
- Badge: "Made for runners by a runner"
- Proof points: "No credit card · No account needed"
- URL: trainpace.com

## Personality

Light, clean, athletic-but-scientific. Emerald is the single hero color; blue appears only as a faint atmospheric wash. Rounded-2xl cards on white with soft shadows. Energetic but credible — data and pace numbers are part of the visual identity (tabular numerals, monospace-feeling stats).

## What NOT to do

- No dark canvas — the brand is light (slate-50/white).
- No additional accent colors (no purple, orange, red except tiny UI dots).
- No heavy grunge/texture; keep it crisp.
