Common Questions

Is TrainPace really free?

Yes. Core features — pace calculator, VDOT calculator, elevation analysis, fuel planning — are free forever. No credit card, no trials, no hidden fees.

Do I need an account?

No! You can use the Pace Calculator, VDOT Calculator, and ElevationFinder without signing up. Create a free account only if you want to save plans.

What is VDOT and how does it work?

VDOT is a running fitness metric from Jack Daniels' Running Formula. Enter any race time and get a score plus training paces for Easy, Marathon, Threshold, Interval, and Repetition zones.

Where do the pace formulas come from?

We use proven exercise science formulas (Jack Daniels' VDOT and Riegel's formula) used by elite coaches worldwide.

How accurate is the fuel planning?

It's based on sports nutrition research recommending 30–60g carbs/hour, tailored to your body weight and goal time.
