🏃 TrainPace

Smarter race training,
from one finish time.

TrainPace is a React + TypeScript web app that turns a single race result into a complete training picture — pace zones, VDOT scores, a week-by-week plan, race-day fueling, and course elevation analysis. Science-backed, mobile-first, and free to start.

The product

Core features

Each feature is a self-contained module (components + hooks + types) that composes into a thin route page. Here's what TrainPace actually does for a runner.

📊

Training Pace Calculator

/calculator

Turn any race time into 7 science-backed training zones, Runna/Strava-style.

  • Easy, Tempo, Max, Speed, Long-run paces + Yasso 800s
  • Goal-time chips auto-calculate; fine-tune slider rewrites time live
  • Live ⚡ VDOT badge updates as you type
  • km ⇄ miles, save plans to your dashboard
📈

VDOT Calculator

/vdot

Jack Daniels VDOT scoring in a clean dashboard layout.

  • Animated gauge + percentile, training zones spectrum
  • "What If" slider re-projects every panel in real time
  • Race-time predictions across distances
  • History saved to localStorage
New
🗓️

Training Plan Generator

/plan

A full week-by-week plan from your goal race, fitness, and available days.

  • Phases: Base → Development → Sharpening → Taper → Race Week
  • Per-day workouts with pace zones, weekly focus, mileage
  • Calendar view + export to iCal (.ics)
  • Paces derived from VDOT / pace results
🥤

Race Fuel Planner

/fuel

Personalized carb, calorie, and hydration strategy for race day.

  • Targets from pace, weight, and race type
  • Google Gemini AI for contextual advice
  • Hourly breakdown — gels, chews, drinks, solids
  • Save & export your plan
🗺️

Course Elevation Analysis

/elevation-finder

Upload a GPX and see exactly what you're up against.

  • Interactive Chart.js elevation profile + grade analysis
  • Mapbox 3D / Leaflet 2D route rendering
  • Algorithmic difficulty scoring
  • Bookmark routes to Firestore + Storage
New
🎯

Goals & Onboarding

/onboarding

One source of truth — your recent race + goal race — captured once.

  • Stored in canonical units (meters + seconds)
  • Fans out to pre-fill Pace, VDOT, Fuel & Dashboard
  • Toggle which tools the goal feeds
  • Goal greeting + settings card
📂

Personal Dashboard

/dashboard

Everything you've saved, synced across devices via Firebase.

  • Route library, pace plans, fuel plans
  • Real-time Firestore queries scoped to the user
  • Search across all data types
  • Thumbnail map previews

poster

Turn a route into a framed, shareable race poster.

  • Mapbox GL route render + HTML Canvas export
  • Custom titles, stats, and styling
  • High-res image download

Blog + Programmatic SEO

/blog · /race · /calculator/:slug

Where it goes

Next steps

A pragmatic roadmap — finishing what's started, then deepening the loop that makes runners come back.

● Near term

Tighten the new core

Wire goals across every tool
Fully connect onboarding's recent/goal race so Pace, VDOT, Fuel & Plan all open pre-filled.
Plan ↔ Calendar sync
One-tap Google/Apple Calendar add beyond the .ics export; show the plan on the dashboard.
Unit tests for the math
Vitest coverage on vdot-math & plan-math to lock in pace/prediction correctness.
Document goals & plan in CLAUDE.md
Both modules exist but aren't in the project docs yet.
● Mid term

Close the training loop

Strava / Garmin import
Auto-pull recent runs to seed goals and GPX routes instead of manual entry/upload.
Adaptive plans
Adjust upcoming weeks based on completed vs. missed workouts and recent fitness.
Heart-rate & power zones
Extend pace zones with HR/power for treadmill and trail running.
Weather-aware fueling
Fold temperature/humidity into hydration and carb targets.
● Long term

Grow & retain

Accounts beyond Google
Add email/password (or passkeys) to widen the funnel past Google OAuth.
Sharing & community
Shareable plan/poster links and race-day pace cards to drive organic growth.
Premium tier
Expand past the poster generator into a clear, sustainable paid plan.
Scale SEO to 100k pages
Use the existing generators + validation to ship race/distance/city pages at volume.
🏁
The throughline: make every input do more work.

TrainPace already turns one race time into many tools. The next chapter is making them talk to each other — goals feeding plans, plans feeding fuel, real runs feeding everything back — so the app becomes a season-long companion, not a one-off calculator.
